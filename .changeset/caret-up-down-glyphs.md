Add `caretUp` (⌃) and `caretDown` (⌄) glyphs to complete the caret family —
previously only `caretLeft` (‹) and `caretRight` (›) existed. Useful for sort
indicators, dropdown toggles, and collapsible-section affordances. The two
arrowhead characters (U+2303 / U+2304) match the weight and wedge form of the
existing horizontal carets.
